How to Use

  1. Make sure your Excel sheet has company names in the first column (A)
  2. Describe the type of companies you want to find
  3. Specify how many companies to add
  4. Click "Search Companies" to find matches
  5. Review the results and click "Add to Excel Sheet"

Note: New companies will be highlighted in green and duplicates will be automatically filtered out.
